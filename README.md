
### How to access the production

[Live app](https://frozen-atoll-04751.herokuapp.com/)

### How to sign in

#### Registered employee ####

1. Enter your e-mail
2. Enter your password
3. Log in

#### Unregistered employee ####

1. Click "Sign up" link
2. Enter your e-mail
3. Enter your password
4. Confirm password
5. Sign up
